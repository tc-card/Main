Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This document explains how TC Cards collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: May 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TC Cards ("we," "our," or "us"). We provide innovative NFC digital business card solutions to help professionals in Tunisia and beyond connect seamlessly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services through our website tccards.tn or our mobile applications.

By accessing or using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number)
  • Professional information (job title, company name, industry)
  • Profile images and custom branding materials
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely by our payment partners)

Usage Data

  • Device information (device type, operating system, browser type)
  • IP address and approximate location data
  • NFC tap interactions and frequency
  • Service usage patterns and preferences

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information to improve user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

Service Provision

To create and manage your digital business card, process transactions, and provide customer support.

Service Improvement

To analyze usage patterns, develop new features, and enhance our services.

Security & Protection

To monitor and prevent security incidents, fraud, and unauthorized access.

Communication

To send service notifications, updates, and marketing communications (with your consent).

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

With trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our service (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing), subject to strict confidentiality agreements.

Business Transfers

In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company.

Legal Requirements

When required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders).

We require all third parties to respect the security of your personal data and to treat it in accordance with the law.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.

Access Controls

Strict role-based access controls limit who can access sensitive data within our organization.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security assessments and penetration testing.

Compliance

We adhere to international data protection standards and Tunisian data protection laws.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@tccards.tn. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information, including personal data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of Tunisia where the data protection laws may differ.

If you are located outside Tunisia and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data, including personal data, to Tunisia and process it there.

We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • privacy@tccards.tn
  • TC Cards, Tunisia
